    About Carmax Auction Site

    Carmax has a long-standing history as the largest automotive retail brand in the UK. Founded in 2002 following the de-merger of Williams PLC,Carmax has grown to represent 21 different vehicle manufacturers in the automotive retail sector.

    Innovation is at the heart of our business and we fully remain committed to transforming automotive retail through digital innovation and operational excellence.

    We operate over 160 sites across the UK and other countries under the brands of Evans Halshaw, Stratstone and CarStore, offering new and used vehicles as well as providing exert aftercare services.

    We also provide innovative software solutions to the automotive industry through Pinewood Technologies, as well as fleet and leasing through Carmax Vehicle Management and wholesale vehicle parts via Quickco.
